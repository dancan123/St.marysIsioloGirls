Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1980

St. Mary's Senior Girls School was founded in 1980 under the sponsorship of the Catholic Diocese of Isiolo. What began as a small community initiative has blossomed into one of the premier academic institutions in the region.

Over the past four decades, we have remained committed to our core mission of providing holistic education to young women, empowering them to become leaders of integrity and agents of positive change in society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in providing holistic education for the empowerment of the girl child for service to humanity.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through faith formation, academic excellence, and integrated human development.
