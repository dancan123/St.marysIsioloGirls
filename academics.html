Curriculum

Holistic & Competitive Learning

St. Mary's Senior Girls School follows the 8-4-4 and CBC curriculum as prescribed by the Ministry of Education, Kenya. Our approach goes beyond textbooks, focusing on critical thinking, practical application, and ethical leadership.

We strictly follow the Kenya National Curriculum, currently transitioning to the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) while maintaining high standards for the 8-4-4 system.
