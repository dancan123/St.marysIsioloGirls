Admission Process

We are delighted that you are considering St. Mary's Senior Girls School for your daughter's secondary education. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward.

1

Online Application

Fill out the digital application form with student details and academic history.

2

Document Submission

Upload KCPE/CBC certificates, birth certificates, and previous school reports.

3

Interview & Assessment

Successful applicants are invited for a physical interview and entrance assessment.

4

Admission Offer

Qualified students receive an official admission letter and fee structure.
